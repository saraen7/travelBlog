Welcome to my travel blog!

My name is Sara and I intend to see it all and share it all.

I'm originally from a small city smack dab in the middle of North America (Minneapolis to be precise). These days I make my homebase in Boston and travel as much as I can.

When I'm not traveling, I'm working as a front end web developer, cooking, reading, exploring Boston on foot and by bike, and watching as much PBS as I have time for.

I've made this blog to share some of my travel experiences and some of where I hope to go next. The blog is divided by continent with pictures and articles about places I've been and one place per continent that I'd like to go next. I'll keep y'all updated on my travels.
